Our conflict mirrors the geopolitical tensions of the time, marked not by overt physical skirmishes but by an enduring ideological struggle against unseen forces. God's Word charges us to recognize the spiritual adversaries seeking to undermine the Kingdom of God.

Stewarding the Spiritual Body of Covenant Believers

Drawing from the wisdom of William Perkins, we delve into the practical dimensions of stewardship in the spiritual realm. As covenantal believers, how do we care for and nurture the body of Christ? Perkins' insights guide us through the challenges and responsibilities of stewarding this precious covenant community.

As the wind of the Spirit blows through the corridors of wisdom, let it carry the fragrance of freedom. Be bold, like the faithful of old, in challenging the status quo. Seek the truths that lie within the Word of God, waiting to be revealed to those with the eyes to see and the hearts to understand.

Just as the alchemist puts on a new paradigm, may The Word of God refine our minds, hearts, and spirits into vessels of honor. Embrace the palette of thought, recognizing that each member is a unique blend of spiritual elements waiting to be harmonized.

Embracing Our Covenantal Heritage

In the spirit of the Scottish Reformed Presbyterian Theological Seminary, CCRPH embraces the rich covenantal heritage that has shaped the Reformed Presbyterian Church. Our commitment to the Westminster Standards undergirds our theological foundation, providing a robust framework for our academic pursuits.

The alchemist sees potential where others see mere substance. In our sacred task of stewarding, recognize the potential within each the spiritual instruments that we are, and the heritage itself. Cultivate an environment where growth is nurtured and potentials are fully realized.

Modeling After the Reformed Presbyterian Church of Scotland

Drawing inspiration from the Reformed Presbyterian Church of Scotland, we aim to cultivate a seminary environment characterized by a deep reverence for God's Word, a passionate commitment to theological precision (as a surgeon wields a scalpel), and a fervent dedication to the principles of the Reformed faith. Our academic Programs reflect the rigorous standards upheld by our Scottish predecessors.

Be attuned to the resonance of the Word of God. Let the voices of the faithful who came before us be heard, inspiring and encouraging those who now walk the halls of our lands.
